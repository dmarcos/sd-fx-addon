Congratulations!

Your browser is now mdns service discovery enabled. Please use navigator.sd for these functions.
  • Use navigator.sd.startDiscovery(target) to look for the desired service, e.g. navigator.sd.startDiscovery('_http._tcp') to look for websites.
  • Use navigator.sd.stopDiscovery() to stop the discovery process.
  • Use navigator.sd.registerService(serviceName, port, options) to advertise your own service.
  • Finally, use registerListener(topic, listener) and removeListener(topic, listener) to listen for 'discoverd' events.
